Tharniel ar Lindeyra

Tharniel is an elven earth mage from the noble House Lindeyra. She is known for her talent in clay golem creation, her noble grace, and her stubborn independence. She left her noble house to pursue her dreams of magical mastery.

Gomi

A small, toddler-named clay golem created by Tharniel during her childhood. Gomi has since grown into a loyal companion.

Hesther

Hesther is a cat beastkin warrior with black fur and golden eyes. After surviving capture and mutilation, she now fights using her wrist stubs, ferocity, and cunning. She speaks curtly, driven by a deep sense of justice.

Kynda

A Stonedwell-born fox-like being with a charcoal scent and blind eyes. He speaks in a mix of old English and Nordic styles, raised with ancient royal traditions. Kynda is a calm strategist, hiding a deeper mythic origin tied to the Fragments and Branch.
